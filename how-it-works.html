How it works

Scale a real recipe, right now.

This is the actual math running inside the app, not a mockup. Change the guest count below and watch every ingredient rescale, with the same unit rounding, station grouping, and cost tracking the product itself uses.

Braised Short Rib for a Crowd
Base recipe: 20 guests, 1x per guest
1

Enter your guest count

Type a number or tap a preset, same as the Scale tab in the real app. No spreadsheet formulas to maintain or break.

2

Every ingredient rescales instantly

Amounts round up to the unit you'd actually buy in, tablespoons become cups, cups become quarts, so nobody's reading a raw decimal on the line. Switch the Imperial/Metric toggle above and every amount converts to kilograms and litres, same as in the app.

3

Print it, station by station

In the real app this same list sorts into a printable, station-grouped prep sheet, and tracks food cost per guest automatically as you scale.
