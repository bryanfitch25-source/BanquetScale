What it does

Built around how a banquet kitchen actually works

Not a recipe blog, not a generic converter — a tool shaped by prep lists, hotel pans, and station sheets.

01

Instant recipe scaling

Enter a guest count and every ingredient recalculates, with smart rounding to the units you actually buy in — dozens, cases, gallons.

02

Station-sorted prep sheets

Generate a printable sheet grouped by Protein, Produce, Dairy, Pantry, and more — handed straight to the line, not reformatted first.

03

Event & menu groups

Build a full banquet menu once, save it as a group, and reuse it across every event at that guest count or a different one.

04

Allergen & dietary tags

Flag gluten, shellfish, dairy, and more per recipe so substitutions and special requests don't get missed on a busy night.

05

Optional team sync

Connect your own free Firebase project to share recipes and events live across every device in the kitchen — or skip it and stay fully offline.

06

Your data, your device

No account required to use it. Nothing leaves your browser unless you choose to turn sync on.

Questions

Before you buy

Is this a subscription?

No. You pay once and keep the file. There's no recurring charge and no license check that can lock you out.

Do I need to install anything?

No. BanquetScale is a single file that opens in any modern browser, on desktop or tablet.

What if I need it on more than one device?

The file works standalone on any device. If you want live syncing between devices — say, the office computer and a tablet on the line — connect your own free Firebase project in a few minutes; setup steps are built into the app.

Where does my data live?

In your browser by default. If you turn on sync, it lives in the Firebase project you control — never on our servers.

What's the difference between the two tiers?

The file and features are identical. Single Kitchen covers one property; Multi-Location covers a catering company running the tool across several.
