What it does

Not a CRM. Not a BEO generator. Just the recipe math, done right.

Most catering software manages bookings, staffing, and invoicing, then still hands you a spreadsheet when it's time to actually scale a recipe. BanquetScale is built for that one job, and it goes deeper than a scaling calculator once you're actually in a kitchen using it.

01

Instant recipe scaling

Change the guest count and every ingredient recalculates on the spot, rounded to how you actually buy: dozens, cases, gallons, not raw decimals nobody can use on the line.

02

Station sorted prep sheets

Print a sheet grouped by Protein, Produce, Dairy, and Pantry, ready to hand straight to the line. No reformatting, no retyping into a template.

03

A full prep timeline, not just a sheet

Pick a saved event and get a day by day countdown built automatically from each recipe's own lead time, all the way through service day.

04

Allergen & dietary tags

Flag gluten, shellfish, dairy, and more per recipe, including Halal Friendly and Kosher Style, so substitutions don't get missed on a busy night.

05

Optional team sync

Connect your own free Firebase project to share recipes and events live across every device in the kitchen, with real admin, editor, and viewer roles, or skip it and stay fully offline.

06

Your data, your device

No account required to use it, and nothing sits on someone else's server by default, unlike most catering platforms, which store your kitchen's data in their cloud whether you like it or not.
